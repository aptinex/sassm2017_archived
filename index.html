The 3rd Biennial Meeting Of South Asian Society for Sexual Medicine (SASSM)

Colombo, Srilanka - 2017



More

Registration

The 3rd Biennial Meeting of South Asian Society for Sexual Medicine (SASSM), will be held in Colombo, Sri Lanka from 19th to 21stÂ November 2017 under the theme of "New Frontiers in Sexual Medicine: mind, body & Science ".

SASSM 2017 brings together experts from all over the world and will once again gather a distinguished faculty to address and discuss the most recent information, controversies and future directions in sexual medicine.

The meeting will include instructional courses, plenaries, symposia, debates, oral presentations and e-posters.

We warmly welcome all those with an interest in the field to join us for SASSM 2017.
